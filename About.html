About US

We work for the empowerment of women, children, and the elderly, as well as the development of urban homeless people.

L.M.S. Charitable Trust is a non-profit, non-political and development organization. It has been working with multi sectoral communities without distinction of caste, creed and community in Bangalore Urban and Kolar Districts. It has been working for development of the most marginalized and vulnerable sections of the society. The basic objective of L.M.S. Charitable Trust is to introduce experiments and innovations in development, economic and employment activities of education, women empowerment and childcare.

Mission & Vision

Our Vision

We foresee a prosperous, harmonious and peaceful society without any discrimination based on caste, creed, culture, economic status or religion.

Our Mission

To facilitate the process of development initiatives in favor of the poor towards achieving all-round prosperity, harmony and peace with equal importance to traditional values, justice and culture. Furthermore, we also work with a balanced perspective between natural resources and legitimate needs of people; through appropriate community based organizations in participation and cooperation with all the stakeholders in a way in which we can gain & sustain self sufficiency and self reliance.

Popular Causes

Women empowerment

L.M.S Charitable Trust has been focusing on empowerment of women, who are marginalized. A few developmental programs such as tailoring training, social analysis training were implemented in Bangalore. 75 women were benefited from these programs. Some of them were supported with placements and a few of them were linked with bank to take up self-employment. Also, vocational trainings are conducted and the women are motivated to undertake the training diligently and pursue a career thereafter. Besides this, awareness programs are conducted on various health issues with focus on HIV and AIDS. Avenues for job placement are also identified in the garment sector. The women are also offered skill training in candle making.

Shelter home for urban homeless

L.M.S. Charitable Trust has been running a night shelter in Hoody Block, Bangalore and the centre has been providing accommodation to homeless people during the nights. The building is being provided by the BBMP, Bangalore, to cater our service to needy urban homeless people. The people who do not have dwelling place come to our centre in the night and we provide them cots, beds, blankets etc. Around 30-35 urban homeless people take shelter in the night. In this reporting period the organization has progressed in its thematic lines and has established programatic interventions in almost all the program lines included in the vision and mission of the organization.

Health Camp

L.M.S. Charitable Trust is dedicated to serving the urban homeless population of Bangalore. Our night shelter in Hoody Block, operating out of a BBMP-provided building, provides nightly accommodation for approximately 30-35 individuals, offering cots, beds, blankets, and crucial access to healthcare through regular health checkups. These checkups are vital to addressing the health challenges faced by those experiencing homelessness. This reporting period has been marked by significant organizational growth, with the establishment of programmatic interventions in nearly all areas outlined in our vision and mission.
